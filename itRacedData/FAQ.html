Frequently Asked Questions

What counties does SJC cover?
Majority of SJC’s research services are provided strictly for the five boroughs of New York City: Kings, Queens, Manhattan, Bronx and Richmond.
Does SJC run any searches in any other counties other than the five located in New York City?
Yes. We can conduct Franchise tax searches, Certificates of Good Standing searches and Bankruptcy searches within the entire state of New York.
Does SJC provide Title Examinations as well as Tax and Municipals?
We do, in all five boroughs of NYC:
Kings, Queens, Manhattan, Bronx and Richmond.
What are SJC’s average turnaround times?
The majority of Tax and Municipal searches can be completed within 24-36 hours. Refinance Title Examinations can be completed within 24-48 hours.
Full 40yr Examinations for most residential properties can be completed within 3 to 4 days. Extensive research may require additional time.
Do I need an account to order?
You don’t, but opening an account is recommended.
How do I open an account?
In our navigation menu, click "My Account", then "Register for an Account" to create an account.
Is SJC integrated with any title software?
Yes, we are integrated with the majority of software available.
How often does SJC invoice clients?
Twice a month, with cycles from 1st through the 15th and the 16th to end of the month.
Does SJC accept Credit Cards?
Yes, all major credit cards are accepted.
What does SJC charge for their services?
Please call 718-852-0051 or email info@sjcarroll.com for a full service and fee list.
