Relief Options for Homeowners Affected by COVID-19 Pandemic

As coronavirus spreads across the United States, so did financial uncertainty for many Americans. If you have been financially affected by the COVID-19 pandemic and unable to make payments on your mortgage, relief options are available for homeowners in need.

THE HOME DEFENDERS

Find Experienced Real Estate Attorneys and Mortgage Relief Experts

At The Home Defenders, we represent your best interests when it comes to knowing all the options available to you, negotiating on your behalf and advising you on the best course of action. Many homeowners who have been hit with a financial setback due to the COVID-19 pandemic are unaware of or unclear on the details surrounding relief options available through the CARE Act. Our team of real estate attorneys and mortgage relief experts is standing by to walk you through the process of securing financial assistance, ensuring your payments are in forbearance, waiving late fees or penalties, and more.

How we help

Discuss Your Unique Situation

Whether your income has been reduced due to illness, job loss or furlough, our team will get to know your circumstances in detail before recommending any available options. Every family’s situation is different and therefore the steps taken to ensure you are able to keep your home also vary. Our team and attorneys are here to find the best solution for you based on your exact needs, how long you expect your income to be reduced, the status of your loan and more.

Assess Your Options

Through the CARE Act, homeowners are already granted a 12-month grace period during which home loans will be on a forbearance status. However, many homeowners may need additional assistance to remain financially stable during the COVID-19 pandemic and its aftermath. Whether you are unable to make full payments on your loan or unable to make any payments for the foreseeable future, our team’s expertise lies in helping find the relief options that will offer you peace of mind.

Represent Your Best Interests

No matter your situation, we are on your side. Our real estate attorneys and team are experienced and skilled in mitigating relationships between homeowners, lenders and loan providers. We represent you and your needs, ensuring outcomes that provide the relief you need during these uncertain and trying times.
